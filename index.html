INTRODUCTION

We are Omni Triad Games. Our goal is to create games that are entertaining for you and for us. We are all passionate about gaming and with that, we intent to bring you the best that we have in store.

The name Omni Triad Games comes from 2 psychological theories, the dark triad and the light triad. The dark triad represents narcissism, psychopathy, and machiavellianism, while the light triad represents a benign, caring, and prosocial orientation toward others.

Hence the name Omni Triad Games means a balance of these two sides, representing us as humans. We all have some degree of dark and light side to us. Which represents the humanity of our studio.
